Case Study: Health Advisory Firm

Building SEO Authority for a Health Advisory Firm: 100% Organic Traffic Growth

Explore how TrueLeafTech partnered with a health advisory firm to establish a dominant online presence, achieving massive organic traffic growth and significantly increasing lead generation through strategic SEO and content marketing.

Client Background

A health advisory firm specializing in corporate wellness programs and individual health coaching sought to expand its digital footprint. Their mission is to empower individuals and organizations with actionable health insights, but their online visibility was limited.

Challenges Faced

Health Advisory Firm faced several critical issues hindering their online growth and lead generation:

  • Low Organic Visibility: Struggled to rank for key health and wellness terms, relying heavily on paid advertising.
  • Limited Content Strategy: Existing content was generic and lacked the depth required to establish thought leadership.
  • Ineffective Lead Capture: Website forms were not optimized for conversion, resulting in low lead generation rates.
  • Competitive Landscape: Operating in a highly competitive digital health space with numerous established players.

TrueLeafTech's Strategic Approach

TrueLeafTech implemented a comprehensive SEO and content marketing strategy focusing on building authority, driving organic traffic, and optimizing lead capture:

  • In-depth Keyword Research: Identified high-intent, long-tail keywords relevant to corporate wellness and health coaching.
  • Authoritative Content Creation: Developed a series of expert articles, whitepapers, and blog posts addressing key health concerns.
