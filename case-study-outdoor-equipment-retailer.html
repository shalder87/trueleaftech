Case Study: Outdoor Equipment Retailer

Driving 167% Growth for an Outdoor Equipment Retailer Through Amazon Catalog Refinement

Explore how TrueLeafTech partnered with a supplier of event gear to overcome Amazon marketplace challenges, achieving remarkable revenue growth and enhanced brand presence through strategic interventions.

Company Profile

A supplier of event gear, including inflatables and tents, managing multiple sub-brands with a wide product range on online marketplaces. Their mission is to provide high-quality, durable equipment for outdoor enthusiasts and event organizers.

Identified Gaps

Outdoor Equipment Retailer faced several critical issues hindering their growth on Amazon:

  • Poor Product Organization: Disorganized product catalog made navigation difficult for customers.
  • Limited Traffic Despite Good Conversions: Despite having good conversion rates for existing traffic, overall traffic was low.
  • Outdated Visuals: Existing product images and A+ Content were outdated and failed to effectively showcase product quality.
  • Underutilized Advertising Opportunities: Advertising campaigns were not fully optimized, leading to inefficient ad spend.

Applied Tactics by TrueLeafTech

TrueLeafTech implemented a multi-faceted strategy focusing on catalog refinement, content enhancement, advertising optimization, and inventory management:

  • Corrected Groupings and Variations: Meticulously audited and refined their entire product catalog for better navigation and user experience.
  • Created Advanced Content and SEO-Optimized Copy: Designed and implemented visually stunning A+ Content and keyword-rich product descriptions.
  • Built Comprehensive Ad Campaigns: Created highly segmented campaigns with seasonal adjustments and continuous bid optimization.
  • Ensured Inventory Health: Implemented proactive measures to maintain optimal inventory levels and compliance through audits and removals.

Growth Metrics

The partnership with TrueLeafTech delivered exceptional growth and established Outdoor Equipment Retailer as a dominant player in the outdoor gear category on Amazon:

  • Monthly Revenue Doubled Year-over-Year: Demonstrating the effectiveness of the implemented strategies.
  • Specific Month Sales Jumped 167%: Significantly boosting revenue and market share.
  • Conversion Rates Improved 50-100%: Indicating highly effective listing optimization and customer engagement.
  • Enhanced Brand Presence: Amazon listings now perfectly reflect their premium brand identity.
  • Optimized Ad Spend: Advertising campaigns became highly efficient, driving increased sales while maintaining a healthy ACOS.
